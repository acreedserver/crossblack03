Installation
To install it simply drop 'Bank Vault' folder into 'garrysmod/addon'.
After you did it, you should customize your Bank Vault in 'garrysmod/addon/Bank Vault/lua/autorun/br_config.lua'.
You also need to setup jobs who can rob the vault and who get paid for guarding vault in 'garrysmod/addon/Bank Vault/lua/entities/br_vault/shared.lua' and in 'garrysmod/addon/Bank Vault/lua/autorun/br_config.lua'.

Use 'vault_spawn <name>' to spawn bank vault at your target position, vault text will be faced to you.
Use 'vault_remove <name>' to remove bank vault.
